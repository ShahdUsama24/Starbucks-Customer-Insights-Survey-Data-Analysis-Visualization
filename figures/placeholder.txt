# ☕ Starbucks Customer Survey — Visual Analysis

A polished, portfolio-ready re-work of an exploratory Jupyter notebook on the
public *Starbucks Customer Satisfaction Survey* (Malaysia, n = 122).

> All visuals follow a consistent Starbucks-inspired, colour-blind-aware palette
> with publication-quality typography and layout.

## 📊 What's inside

| # | Visual | Insight |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Correlation heatmap | Service / Ambiance / Quality form one cluster; spend tracks visit frequency |
| 2 | Age-group donut | 70 % of respondents are Young Adults (20-29) |
| 3 | Ratings × age group | Adults (30-39) are the happiest cohort; WiFi underperforms everywhere |
| 4 | Spend × age (box+strip) | Spend rises monotonically with age |
| 5 | Visit-frequency mix × age | Adults (30-39) hold the only meaningful weekly-visitor share |
| 6 | Loyalty × age | Mature Adults are the most loyal segment |
| 7 | Employment status | Sample dominated by students + employed professionals |
| 8 | Duration heatmap (freq × type) | Dine-in stays 2-3× longer than take-away |
| 9 | Rating distributions (small multiples) | WiFi & Price have unhappy tails |
| 10 | Loyalty donut | ~80 % plan to keep buying |
| 11 | Loyal vs Non-Loyal ratings | Quality / Service / Ambiance show the biggest loyalty gap |
| 12 | Confusion matrices (LR / RF / XGB) | LR generalises best on this small sample |
| 13 | Model accuracy comparison | Logistic Regression: **88 %** test accuracy |

## 🎨 Design choices

- **Brand-anchored palette**: Starbucks green (#00704A), gold (#CBA258), brown (#5A3A22).
- **Colour-blind aware**: diverging green↔orange for correlations, single-hue greens for
  sequential heatmaps, green/red only used for binary Yes/No (loyalty).
- **Removed clutter**: no top/right spines, light grid, consistent title weight & colour.
- **Annotated bars / pies** so figures are readable without consulting the table.
